üîç How Bitcoin Actually Works

Think of Bitcoin as a digital ledger that everyone can see, but no one can cheat. Here's the simple version:

1. You Want to Send Bitcoin

You create a transaction saying "Send 1 Bitcoin from Address A to Address B"

2. Network Verification

Thousands of computers verify you actually own that Bitcoin

3. Block Creation

Your transaction gets bundled with others into a "block"

4. Permanent Record

The block gets added to the blockchain - a permanent, unchangeable record
